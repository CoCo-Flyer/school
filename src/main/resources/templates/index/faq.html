Do I need permission from my home school?

No, you do not need permission from your home school. All you need is proof of completion of the prerequisites for your desired course.

Does my credit count towards my high school diploma?

Yes, all credits received from Willowdale Online High School are recognized as Ontario based credits. They are counted towards your Ontario Secondary School Diploma and University/College application.

How does a course work?

Every course has lessons, homework, quizzes, assignments and tests. All the lessons are materials or videos based. You will submit your solutions and written assignments online based on your own schedule. You can also contact your teacher by email if you have questions. Any tests or quizzes will be completed online, open-book and timed. The final exam is also completed online under the supervision of an appropriate proctor. The student will be responsible for selecting a proctor and submitting an approval application to Willowdale Online High School. The date, time, and location of the exam will be mutually agreed upon by the student and proctor.

How long does it take to complete a course?

We do not have a set start and finish time, but we recommend you to complete your course within 4 months. Students who delay the completion of their course often don't finish it. It is in your best interest to schedule your learning so that your course can be completed in a reasonable amount of time. Your teacher will monitor your progress to make sure you are staying on a proper schedule.

What is your refund policy?

There are no tuition refunds. If a student has completed less than 1 unit of the course, it is possible to transfer courses.
